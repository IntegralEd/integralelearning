Our Services

We provide comprehensive eLearning development and instructional design services tailored to education organizations.

Instructional Design

Research-based learning design for online and blended environments that prioritizes learner engagement and authentic outcomes.

Articulate Storyline Development

Advanced Storyline courses with branching scenarios, interactive simulations, and custom interactions that go beyond templates.

Articulate Rise Development

Mobile-first, responsive Rise courses for rapid deployment and consistent learner experiences across devices.

Media Design & Instructional Video

Custom graphics, animations, and instructional videos designed to clarify complex concepts and maintain learner attention.

LMS Administration & Support

Ongoing support for course catalog management, user administration, and platform optimization to keep your learning ecosystem running smoothly.

Our Approach

A proven five-step process that ensures your learning solutions are instructionally sound, technically excellent, and aligned with your goals.

1

Discovery

We learn about your learners, goals, constraints, and existing materials through stakeholder interviews and needs analysis.

2

Learning Design

We create instructional blueprints that define learning objectives, assessment strategies, and interaction patterns before production begins.

3

Development

We build your courses using Storyline, Rise, or custom tools, with attention to accessibility, performance, and visual design.

4

Review & Iteration

We incorporate your feedback through structured review cycles, refining content and interactions until they meet your standards.

5

Launch & Ongoing Support

We deploy your courses to your LMS and provide ongoing support for updates, maintenance, and continuous improvement.
