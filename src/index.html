How We Work

A rigorous, learner-centered process that balances instructional research with the practical realities of your organization and learners.

1

Listen and Learn

We begin by understanding learner context, goals, constraints, and success criteria through interviews with stakeholders and subject matter experts. When relevant, we engage with national experts and review existing materials, research, and instructional context to ground our work in best practices informed by learning theory.

2

Case Studies and Characters

We design realistic case studies and learn-along characters grounded in authentic learner contexts. These scenarios reflect the real decisions, challenges, and trade-offs learners face, establishing narrative structure before we move into detailed interaction design.

3

Storyboarding

We create instructional storyboards that outline interactions, assessments, and the learner's journey. This phase ensures alignment between objectives and activities, helping stakeholders and instructional designers identify potential gaps or challenges before development begins.

5

Development

We build courses with meticulous attention to visual design, user experience, and accessibility. Through thoughtfully crafted scenarios and interactions, we create engaging learning experiences that drive engagement and retention.

6

Review and Iteration

Through structured review cycles, we refine content, interactions, and media based on your feedback. This collaborative process ensures the final product aligns with your vision and the needs of your learners.
