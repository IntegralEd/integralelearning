How We Work

A rigorous, learner-centered process that balances instructional research with the practical realities of your organization and learners.

1

Listen and Learn

We begin by understanding learner context, goals, constraints, and success criteria through interviews with stakeholders and subject matter experts. When relevant, we engage with national experts and review existing materials, research, and instructional context to ground our work in what actually works.

2

Case Studies and Characters

We design realistic case studies and learn-along characters grounded in authentic learner contexts. These scenarios reflect the real decisions, challenges, and trade-offs learners face, establishing narrative structure before we move into detailed interaction design.

3

Learning Design

We create instructional blueprints that define learning objectives, assessment strategies, and interaction patterns. This design phase ensures alignment between what learners need to do and how we'll support them before production begins.

4

Development

We build courses using Storyline, Rise, or custom tools, with careful attention to accessibility, performance, and visual design. Development follows the approved design, with regular check-ins to ensure we're on track.

5

Review and Iteration

We incorporate your feedback through structured review cycles, refining content, interactions, and media until they meet your standards. This collaborative process ensures the final product aligns with your vision and learner needs.

6

Launch and Ongoing Support

We deploy your courses to your LMS, provide documentation and training for your team, and remain available for updates, maintenance, and continuous improvement as your program evolves.
